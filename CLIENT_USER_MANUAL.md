# PART 1 — WEB DASHBOARD

---

## Step 1 — Open the Web Dashboard

1. Open your browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended)
2. Go to the URL provided to you
3. You will see the SKNLP login page

---

## Step 2 — Log In

1. Enter your **Email:** `admin@sknlp.campaign365.app`
2. Enter your **Password:** `password`
3. Click **Log In**
4. You will be taken to the main Dashboard

---

## Step 3 — Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your home screen. It shows:

- **Voters Contacted** — how many voters your team has spoken to
- **Total Voters** — total voters in your database
- **Active Campaigns** — how many campaigns are currently running
- **Canvassers Online** — how many canvassers are active right now

> The dashboard updates automatically. Refresh the page to see the latest numbers.

---

## Step 4 — Voters Module

**Where to find it:** Click **"Voters"** in the left sidebar

**What you can do:**
1. See the full list of all 180+ registered voters
2. Click any voter's name to see their full profile
3. Filter voters by Constituency, Sentiment (Supporter / Undecided / Opposition), or Age Group
4. Click **"Add Voter"** to manually add a new voter
5. Click **"Import"** to upload a CSV file of voters in bulk

**Voter Profile shows:**
- Personal details (name, address, phone, email)
- Constituency and Polling Division
- Support Level (Supporter / Undecided / Opposition)
- Contact history

---

## Step 5 — Campaigns Module

**Where to find it:** Click **"Campaigns"** in the left sidebar

**What you can do:**
1. See all active, upcoming, and completed campaigns
2. Click a campaign to see its details
3. View how many voters have been targeted and how many responded

**Current Campaign:** 2026 General Election — National (Active)

---

## Step 6 — Canvassing (Field Operations)

**Where to find it:** Click **"Canvassing"** in the left sidebar

**What you can see:**
1. **Walk Lists** — the 3 current canvassing zones:
   - St. Christopher North — Zone A (88% complete — 280/320 doors)
   - St. Christopher North — Zone B (100% complete — Finished)
   - Basseterre Central Walk (44% complete — 180/410 doors)
2. How many doors have been knocked today
3. Which canvassers are assigned to each zone

---

## Step 7 — Team Management

**Where to find it:** Click **"Team"** in the left sidebar

**What you can do:**
1. See all team members (Canvassers, Runners, Outdoor Agents)
2. Add a new team member — click **"Add Member"**, fill in their details
3. Assign roles to team members
4. Reset a team member's password

---

## Step 8 — Communications

**Where to find it:** Click **"Communications"** in the left sidebar

**What you can do:**
1. Send an **Email** to all supporters or a specific group
2. Send an **SMS** message to voters
3. View all previously sent campaigns

**To send a message:**
1. Click **"New Campaign"**
2. Choose Email or SMS
3. Write your message
4. Select your audience (All Voters / Supporters Only / Undecided)
5. Click **"Send Now"**

---

## Step 9 — Runners (Logistics)

**Where to find it:** Click **"Runners"** in the left sidebar

**What you can see:**
1. All runners and their current GPS location on the map
2. Runner name, current task, and estimated arrival time
3. Assign a pickup task to a runner

---

## Step 10 — GOTV (Get Out The Vote)

**Where to find it:** Click **"GOTV"** in the left sidebar

**Use this on Election Day to:**
1. See live voter turnout numbers
2. View which polling stations have high/low turnout
3. Send a final push message to all volunteers
4. Assign runners to areas with low turnout

---

## Step 11 — Reports & Analytics

**Where to find it:** Click **"Reports"** in the left sidebar

**What you can see:**
1. Voter Contact Rate
2. Canvassing Efficiency
3. Turnout Projection
4. Fundraising Progress
5. Sentiment breakdown (Supporter / Undecided / Opposition chart)

Click **"Export"** to download any report as a PDF or Excel file.

---

## Step 12 — Fundraising

**Where to find it:** Click **"Fundraising"** in the left sidebar

**What you can do:**
1. See total amount raised vs. goal
2. View top donors list
3. Add a new donation — click **"Add Donation"**
4. See donation trends over time (chart)

---

## Step 13 — Notifications

**Where to find it:** Click the **bell icon** at the top right

**Shows:**
- Urgent alerts (red)
- Canvassing updates
- New donations
- Runner assignments

Click **"Mark All Read"** to clear notifications.

---

## Step 14 — Profile & Settings

**Where to find it:** Click your name at the top right → **"Profile"**

**What you can do:**
1. Update your name and email
2. Change your password
3. View your party branding (colors, logo)
4. Toggle email and push notification preferences

---

## Step 15 — Log Out

Click your name at the top right → **"Log Out"**

Your session is saved — next time you open the app, you will stay logged in automatically.

# PART 2 — MOBILE APP (Android)

---

## Step 1 — Install the App

1. You will receive a download link via WhatsApp or email
2. Open the link on your Android phone
3. Tap **"Download APK"**
4. If your phone asks "Allow install from unknown sources" → tap **Allow**
5. Tap **Install**
6. The app icon **"Campaign 365"** will appear on your home screen

---

## Step 2 — Log In

1. Open the app
2. Enter **Email:** `admin@sknlp.campaign365.app`
3. Enter **Password:** `password`
4. Tap **Sign In**
5. You will see the Home Screen

> **Tip:** Tap one of the quick-fill buttons at the bottom of the login screen to automatically fill in your credentials.

---

## Step 3 — Home Screen

The Home Screen shows:
- **Your name and role** at the top
- **Live stats:** Voters Contacted, Doors Today, Active Canvassers
- **Map** of your turf area
- **Turf Cards** showing your assigned walk lists and progress
- **Quick Access buttons** at the bottom to jump to any screen

---

## Step 4 — Start Canvassing

1. From the bottom tab bar, tap **"Canvass"** (map icon)
2. You will see the active canvassing map with your current area
3. The next voter's name and address appear at the bottom
4. After speaking with the voter, select their **support level:**
   - **Support** — they support SKNLP
   - **Undecided** — not sure yet
   - **Oppose** — they do not support
5. Tap **"Save & Next Voter"** to record the visit and move on

---

## Step 5 — Record a Voice Note

On the Canvassing screen:
1. Tap the **microphone button** (🎤) at the bottom
2. Speak your notes about the voter
3. Tap the microphone again to stop
4. The voice note is saved with the voter record

---

## Step 6 — Mark Voter as "Not Home"

On the Canvassing screen:
1. Tap **"Not Home"** button next to the voter's name
2. The visit is recorded as "Not Home" and you move to the next voter

---

## Step 7 — Quick Add a New Voter

If you meet someone who is not in the system:
1. From Home Screen → tap **"➕ Quick Add Voter"**
2. Fill in their name, address, phone number
3. Select their support level
4. Tap **"Save & Add to Turf"**
5. They will be added to the voter database immediately

---

## Step 8 — View Canvassing History

1. From Home Screen → tap **"📜 Canvass History"**
2. You will see all your previous canvassing sessions
3. Each session shows: date, location, number of voters contacted, and sync status

---

## Step 9 — View Voter Profile

1. From Home Screen → tap **"👤 Voter Profile"**
2. Search for any voter by name
3. View their full details: address, support level, contact history, notes

---

## Step 10 — Quick Reports

1. From Home Screen → tap **"📊 Quick Reports"**
2. You will see:
   - Doors knocked this week
   - Voters persuaded (now supporting SKNLP)
   - Support trend chart (last 4 days)
   - Voter sentiment breakdown (pie chart)
3. Tap **"Export Report"** to share the summary

---

## Step 11 — Runner Tracking (Supervisors only)

1. From Home Screen → tap **"🏃 Runner Tracking"**
2. See all runners' live GPS locations on the map
3. Tap a runner to see their name, current task, and ETA
4. Tap **"Contact Runner"** to call them
5. Tap **"Request Backup"** to send another runner

---

## Step 12 — GOTV Mode (Election Day)

1. From Home Screen → tap **"🗳️ GOTV Mode"**
2. Toggle **"Activate GOTV"** to ON
3. You will see:
   - Priority voters who haven't voted yet (with phone numbers)
   - Live heat map showing turnout by area
   - Your vote target and current progress
4. Tap **"Call Now"** next to any voter to call them directly
5. Tap **"Send Reminder"** to send them a text message

---

## Step 13 — AI Insights

1. From Home Screen → tap **"🤖 AI Insights"**
2. The AI will show you:
   - Which voter groups are most persuadable
   - Recommended next actions
   - Sentiment analysis of your canvassing data
   - Turnout predictions by constituency

---

## Step 14 — My Schedule

1. From Home Screen → tap **"📅 My Schedule"**
2. See your assigned tasks for each day of the week
3. Tap **"Start Now"** on any task to begin
4. Completed tasks are marked automatically

---

## Step 15 — Report an Issue

If you encounter a hostile voter or an emergency situation:
1. Tap **"⚠️ Report Issue"**
2. Select the type of issue (Hostile Voter, Safety Concern, Technical Issue, etc.)
3. Add notes about what happened
4. Tap **"Submit Report"**
5. Your cluster manager will be notified immediately

---

## Step 16 — Panic Button (EMERGENCY ONLY)

**Use this only in a real emergency.**

1. From Home Screen → tap **"🚨 Panic Screen"**
2. A 10-second countdown will start
3. If you do NOT cancel within 10 seconds, an **emergency alert is automatically sent** to HQ with your GPS location
4. To cancel: tap **"Cancel"** before the countdown reaches zero

---

## Step 17 — Election Night Live Results

On Election Night:
1. Tap **"🗳 Election Night"** from the Home Screen
2. See live results as they come in, constituency by constituency
3. Results update in real time
4. Color-coded map shows which areas SKNLP is winning

---

## Step 18 — Recruit a Volunteer

1. Tap **"🤝 Recruit Volunteer"** from the Home Screen
2. Fill in the new volunteer's details
3. Select their availability and skills
4. Tap **"Sign Up"**
5. They will receive an invitation to join the team

---

## Step 19 — Sync Your Data

All data is saved automatically on your phone even without internet.

**When you reconnect to the internet:**
- Data syncs automatically in the background
- You will see a green badge "Offline Mode Ready" on the Home Screen when you're offline

**To manually sync:**
1. Tap your profile (👤) at the bottom right
2. Tap **"Log Out & Sync Data"**
3. All data syncs to the cloud
4. You will see a "Sync Complete ✓" screen with the number of records uploaded

---

## Step 20 — Log Out

1. Tap **"Profile"** tab (👤) at the bottom
2. Scroll down and tap **"Log Out"**
3. Your data is automatically synced before logout

# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What if I have no internet connection?**
A: The app works fully offline. Everything you record is saved on your phone. When you reconnect, it syncs automatically.

**Q: Can two people log in with the same account?**
A: No. Each canvasser should have their own account. Contact your cluster manager to get individual accounts created.

**Q: How do I add more team members?**
A: On the web dashboard → Team → Add Member. They will receive login credentials by email.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. A reset link will be sent to you.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely in the Campaign 365 cloud. Only your party admins can access your data.

**Q: How do I know if my data has synced?**
A: Go to Profile → "Offline Sync Status". It shows the last sync time. A green dot means you are online and synced.
